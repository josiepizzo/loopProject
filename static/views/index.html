By The Numbers

Why We Need Women In Technology.

  • Early 1980's is when Women were most interested in Computer Science.

    37%

    Of Computer Science majors were women. In 1984 that percentage flattened and continued to drop.

    NPR

  • By 2012 Women Studying Computer Science dropped

    18%

    The number of women studying computer science plunged. "In a study Google released last month, the company surveyed about 1,600 men and women. It showed that girls aren't really taught what computer science actually means, and are half as likely to be encouraged to study it."

    TechRepublic.com

  • In 2014

    75% | 58%

    Women WITH a Sponsor show 2% more satisfaction over Men with rate of advancement and Promotions. And 58% of Women with NO Sponsor, 57% of Men with NO Sponsor are satisfied with rate of advancement and promotions.

    Re-rendered from Hewlett et al., 2014

  • In 2015 we see somewhat of an increase.

    25%

    The number of women in the computing workforce increased, but not significantly & still LESS than 10% of those women are women of color!

    NCWIT.ORG
